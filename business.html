ABOUT US


Black Diamond Limo, LLC would like to personally thank you for inquiring about our services. We can assure you that you will not be displeased with any of the services that we provide. Our company takes pride in providing the highest quality transportation in the area as well as all surrounding areas. Our growing fleet is operated with you in mind from start to park.

Black Diamond Limo, LLC aims to please and we are open to any request that you may have. Our limos and shuttle vehicles are all equipped to handle most any request that comes our way. We encourage you to contact us today and give Black Diamond Limo, LLC the opportunity to meet your transport needs. With competitive rates, rare fleet vehicles, and exceptional service we guarantee 100% customer satisfaction
